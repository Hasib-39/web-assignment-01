Idea, innovation, vision and challenges in New Bangladesh

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embracing cutting-edge technologies and fostering creativity to drive Bangladesh towards a knowledge-based economy, enhancing productivity and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Prioritizing green practices and renewable energy to ensure environmental preservation, economic resilience, and a sustainable future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Developing world-class physical and digital infrastructure to support urbanization, economic growth, and improved connectivity across the nation.

Empowerment

Equipping individuals with skills, education, and opportunities to unlock their potential, fostering inclusive growth and a more equitable society.

The Vision of Future

A future driven by innovation, inclusivity, and sustainability, where technology and human potential converge to create a prosperous, equitable, and resilient society. This vision emphasizes smart infrastructure, renewable energy, education for all, and opportunities that empower individuals to thrive while preserving the planet for generations to come.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

“When we want to help the poor, we usually offer them charity. Most often we use charity to avoid recognizing the problem and finding the solution for it. Charity becomes a way to shrug off our responsibility. But charity is no solution to poverty. Charity only perpetuates poverty by taking the initiative away from the poor. Charity allows us to go ahead with our own lives without worrying about the lives of the poor. Charity appeases our consciences.”

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
